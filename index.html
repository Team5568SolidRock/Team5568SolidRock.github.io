What is FIRST?

For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology (FIRST) is an international youth organization that operates the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) , FIRST LEGO League (FLL), FIRST LEGO League Jr. (FLLJ) , and FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) competitions.

Who are we?

We are a FIRST robotics team based in Bentonville Arkansas. We provide a way to learn how to build robots, solve complex problems, and work as a team. We believe in coopertition and working hard to achieve our best.
